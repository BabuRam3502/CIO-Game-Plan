Reading & Note-Taking Guide

As you read, don't just absorb information. Actively seek to understand the **why** behind the text. Use these questions to guide your focus and note-taking:

From Business Publications:

  • **What business problem is being solved?** (e.g., revenue issue, market share problem, etc.)
  • **How is technology being used to solve it?** (e.g., a new app, AI initiative)
  • **What is the financial impact?** (Look for keywords like "cost savings" or "revenue growth")
  • **Who is making the decision?** (e.g., CEO, CFO, CIO)

From Books (*Radical Candor*):

  • Identify specific examples in the book that you can relate to your own experiences.
  • Note down key frameworks or concepts (e.g., "Care Personally, Challenge Directly").
  • Write down a plan for how you can apply one concept in your next meeting.
